About


We believe in making youth robotics education affordable and accessible for everyone.

Our Classes

Face it, automation, software, and robots are all around us. We live in a world packed with tech, computers, and ‘smart’ devices. We teach youth how to build and program robots with class offerings such as Robotics Foundations and Electronics and Sensing.

Our Students

A big part of Learn Robotics is overcoming the learning curve of beginning robot programming. We pride ourselves on making classes engaging, interactive, and informative for students of all levels and backgrounds.
